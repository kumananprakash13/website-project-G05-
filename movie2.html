Main Character

Guido Orefice – a loving and optimistic father who uses humor and creativity to protect his son in a concentration camp.

Movie Summary

Life Is Beautiful (1997) is an Italian film set during World War II. Guido turns a tragic situation into a “game” to shield his son from the horrors of a Nazi concentration camp. His optimism and courage inspire hope even in the darkest times.

Life Lesson Learned

The movie taught me that love, hope, and sacrifice are more powerful than fear. Even in extreme hardship, positivity and protecting others can make a life-changing difference.
